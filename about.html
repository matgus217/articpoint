1995

THIS IS US

The resturant was built in 1989 and started as an art gallery. Today, 34 years later we are here, ARTIC POINT. We are the only ones to work with arctic natural food of first class, directly from the Torne River.

1997

THE RED THREAD

We get a building permit and starts to redo the art gallery into a resturant. Our focus from the beginning was the theme, ice and snow. And to take care of the unique environment found in Jukkasjärvi.

2000

THE GRAND OPENING AND THE NATURE IN MIND

In 2000 we opened with flying colours. Ice and snow were everywhere to be found and it was a success. We are passionate about creating life-enriching experiences and we live with nature in everything we do. All the food is organic and grown with love from our nearby farms and fields.

2012-
TODAY

ICE ART AND AN ATTRACTION IN SWEDEN – ALL YEAR ROUND

Art is present everywhere in the resturant. At Artic Point you enjoy local delicacies, the silence and If you're lucky, you'll see the northern lights dancing in the sky during the dark months. We are fortunate to be able to do this 365 days a year. We focus on two things: A climate-smart restaurant that gives you as a customer delicacies from northern Sweden. And that thing that makes us unique, snow and ice.
